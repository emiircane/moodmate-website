Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

MoodMate is designed to respect your privacy. We believe that your relationship is personal, and your data should be too.

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to facilitate the connection between you and your partner. This includes your User ID (generated anonymously), your current mood status, and device signals you choose to share (Battery level, Focus Mode status).

2. Data Usage

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Synchronizing your status with your linked partner.
  • Sending push notifications for mood updates.
  • Improving app stability and performance.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

Data Control

You have full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our servers, you may do so at any time. This action is irreversible.

How do I pair with my partner?
