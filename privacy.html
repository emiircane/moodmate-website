Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is MoodMate's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent.

  • Mock Data: Currently, MoodMate is designed for couples to share moods. We store mood data, battery status, and focus mode status to synchronize between partners.
  • Device Info: We access device information such as battery level and Focus Mode (Do Not Disturb) status only to share it with your connected partner.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Synchronize your mood and status with your partner in real-time.
  • Send push notifications when your partner updates their status.
  • Improve the app experience and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we'll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

5. Your Rights

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services. You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
