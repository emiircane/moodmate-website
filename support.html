Common Questions

How do I pair with my partner?
Open the app, tap 'Pairing', and enter your partner's unique code shown on their screen.
Why isn't the battery status updating?
Battery status updates automatically every 20 minutes to save power. Ensuring both devices have internet connection helps synchronization.
How do I delete my account?
You can request account deletion by emailing us at the address above. We will process it immediately.
